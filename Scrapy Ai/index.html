Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this website?

This website helps users discover websites related to their learning goals. We use search engine technology and (potentially) AI-powered recommendations (coming soon) to suggest websites that can be helpful for your learning journey..

How does it work?

You enter a search query describing what you want to learn. Our search engine finds websites that match your keywords. Additionally, in the future, we plan to integrate with Bard, a large language model from Google AI, to analyze your search query and the content of suggested websites for more personalized recommendations

What kind of websites does it recommend?

The website recommends websites that can be helpful for learning purposes, such as educational websites, tutorials, online courses, or websites with information on specific skills or topics

What is the chatbot for?

The chatbot is an additional resource to help you find answers to frequently asked questions, navigate the platform's features, and access learning recommendations through a conversational interface.

Can the chatbot personalize recommendations?

Currently, the chatbot provides general recommendations based on your keywords and learning goals. In the future, with advancements in our chatbot's capabilities, it might be able to personalize recommendations based on your interactions and learning preferences.

Are there limitations to the chatbot?

The chatbot is still under development, and it might not be able to answer complex inquiries. We are constantly working to improve its capabilities based on user feedback.
